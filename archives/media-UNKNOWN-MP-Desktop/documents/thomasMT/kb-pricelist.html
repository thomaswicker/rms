RMS Keyboards Price List

Note: RMS Keyboards is the technology behind the MTI brand Keyboard Patch Solutions and the R&H brand KeyboardEase. You may refer to this guide for prices on these products.

The price for RMS Keyboards is based on two factors:

  • the amount of programming involved in the given show; and
  • the length of your run.

The guide below is the minimum license price, reflecting performance runs of up to two weeks. Longer runs are subject to an additional $50/performance week.

Once purchased, you can use the program on as many computers as there are keyboard parts in the show, plus one additional for backup. You also get an unlimited amount of rehearsal time, provided you have an executed performance license. See this FAQ for more information about duration of usage.
