Sharing Content With Your Cast

If you are the person who first requested the unlock code (and you're still on the same computer used to make that request), you'll be able to unlock right away. But before your cast and production team can use RMS Mix, you will first need to share your show with them. You do this by exporting to the RMS Cloud.

  1. The original person who requested the code should start by unlocking his or her desktop computer. If that's you, skip ahead to the next section and then come back. There needs to be at least one unlocked computer before you can distribute everything.
  2. When you first exported your show from Sinfonia or RMS Coach, you were asked if you wanted to upload your content to the RMS Cloud. If you went forward with this, you can skip to the next step. If not, from within RMS Mix, select menu Tools --> Export to RMS Cloud.
  3. See Exporting from Sinfonia or Exporting from RMS Coach if you want to share additional, alternate versions of your show. You might want to do this if, for example, the show is double cast and the leads perform in different keys. Or if you want to customize various rehearsal configurations with different edits.
  4. Tell everyone to download the show. See Download RMS Mix Content to a Desktop Computer or Download RMS Mix Content to a Mobile Device.
  5. You can export up to five versions of your show as part of your basic RMS Mix license. Your cast and team can download these an unlimited number of times on an unlimited number of devices during your rehearsal and production period.
