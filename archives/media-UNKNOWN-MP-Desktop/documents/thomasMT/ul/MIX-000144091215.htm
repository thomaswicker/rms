Thank you very much for using RMS Mix. Your order has been processed. RMS Mix is a sophisticated resource that can be used in many ways on many different platforms. In order to get the most out of it, please carefully review this entire document.

The document is broken down into the following sections:

  • Your Unlock Credentials
  • Unlocking The First Desktop Computer
  • Sharing Content
  • Unlocking Everything Else (Smartphones, Tablets, Additional Computers)
  • Other Important Notes

Your Unlock Credentials

First and foremost, here are the details you need to unlock:

  • Order Number: CCH-005246
  • Unlock Code: 04889EB8363E57997C0D8205D16BA7FE

Alternatively, you can unlock with the following QR code. With the exception of the first desktop unlock, it will probably be more convenient to unlock with the QR in the majority of cases.

Unlocking The First Desktop Computer

This applies to the computer that was originally used to submit a purchase/request for RMS Mix.

  1. Click the Authenticate button at program launch.
  2. If asked, fill in any missing user information. (It should already be filled in from when you initially submitted the unlock request.)
  3. Click Unlock.
  4. Copy and paste your unlock code into the Unlock Code screen.
  5. Click Okay.

Your copy of RMS Mix should now be unlocked. See this manual link for more details.

Sharing Content

You will most likely have customized content to share with your cast and production team. Modified tempos, transpositions, cuts, level adjustments, etc. This is especially true if you are using RMS Mix in tandem with a Sinfonia and/or RMS Coach booking. But even if you purchased RMS Mix as a standalone product, you will likely have some access to Sinfonia to tailor the resource to your needs. To get this content to everyone involved with your production, you export it to the RMS Cloud.

  1. See Exporting from Sinfonia or Exporting from RMS Coach to share alternate versions of your show. You might want to create multiple versions if, for example, the show is double cast and the leads perform in different keys. Or if you want to customize various rehearsal configurations with different edits.
  2. Tell everyone to download RMS Mix on their computers and/or mobile devices.
  3. You can export up to five versions of your show as part of your basic RMS Mix license. Your cast and team can download these an unlimited number of times on an unlimited number of devices during your rehearsal and production period.

Unlocking Everything Else (Smartphones, Tablets, Additional Computers)

These instructions are for the vast majority of RMS Mix users. It involves using the QR code supplied with the unlock credentials above.

iPhone, iPad, Android

  1. If you haven't done so already, download the app from the Apple App Store or Google Play. You can find direct links on our web site here.
  2. From the app, tap the Show Management button
  3. Tap the + button to add new content (upper right corner).
  4. If this is your first time using the app, you will be asked to fill out your name and email address and tap the next arrow to proceed.
  5. Tap Scan QR image.
  6. The app will now access your device's camera so you may scan the QR. Simply point your device at the QR image, either on a separate screen or on a paper print out. As soon as the QR is read, you will be unlocked. You can download and use the content for the duration of your license period.

Additional Windows and Macintosh OSX Computers

  1. If you haven't done so already, download the app from our web site.
  2. If you already have show content on your computer, click the Run Trial Version at launch to enter the trial version of whatever show happens to be there.
  3. If there's no show content present at launch, you will be asked to get some either from Show Management or the Show Delivery Utility. Click on Show Management and skip the next step.
  4. From the trial version, select menu Tools > Show Management.
  5. Click the + button to add new content (upper right corner).
  6. Enter your name and email if this hasn't been done already.
  7. Click Import QR Image.
  8. You will now be asked to select the QR image file locally. If you don't have this, you can get it by right clicking the image above and selecting one of the Save Image options. We recommend saving to a convenient location, such as the Desktop.
  9. As soon as the QR image is imported, you will be unlocked and can use the program for the duration of your license.

Other Ways To Unlock

  • It's possible to unlock without a QR code. Simply click or tap the appropriate 'No QR' button and manually enter the credentials when prompted.
  • It's also possible to import a QR image into a mobile device. To capture a QR image so that it's available locally, tap and hold the image when viewing this page from your device. This will bring up an option to save the QR image to your device - and then you can import it to unlock.

Other Important Notes

  • Your unlock code will expire on November 23, 2015.
  • Assuming you have booked at least a few weeks in advance of your first performance, you will be able to create and share five different versions of your show. More exports may be purchased, if necessary. Regardless, there is no limit on the number of times you can download this content. You, the cast, and the rest of the production team can put it on as many computers, smartphones, and tablets as you want. We encourage you to spread the word and get everyone involved with the show to use it.
  • Feel free to contact us if you need any assistance. We weant you to have an excellent experience with our resources.

Have a great show!!
